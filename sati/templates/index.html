How real are your fears?

We all feel overwhelmed with negative emotions from time to time. Fear of unknown or fear of future often takes a toll. Have we ever wondered how real our fears are? The Sati app gives us an opportunity to get a reality check on our thoughts- bridging the gap between our fearful imaginations and how things really are. Various features allow us to note our thoughts, imaginations and and be able to visualize our mind data patterns as they are. One of the most popular methods in behavioral therapy CBT allows us to modify our thought patterns from negative to positive. This approach is different in the sense it allows us to see real evidence or verification of how unrealistic our thinking is. Thus we can slowly train our minds learning see thoughts as 'just thoughts' and not take them too seriously.

Thought postits! Imagination vs reality

Journal your fear when it arises and then validate your concerns later- You are worried about presenting your pitch to investors. You go online and write in as much detail about your worrying thoughts for that day. Then you give your pitch on the destined day and write about how it actually went. Finally, you qualitatively compare your thoughts side by side.
